Veg omlette

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
onions 1
cabbage 1/2 cup finely chopped
carrot 1
beans 2 Tbsp
turmeric powder pinch
salt as required
pepper powder as required
green chillies 1

PROCEDURE

Step 1: Take a mixing bowl.beat the two eggs in it.
Step 2: Add chopped onions,cabbage,carrot,green chillies to it.
Step 3: Mix it well.Add salt,turmeric powder and pepper powder and mix again.
Step 4: Take a pan,apply some oil to it.
Step 5: Put this batter on the pan.keep a lid on the pan.
Step 6: cook for 5-10 mts.apply some oil if needed.
Step 7: slowly turn to other side to cook.it is ready to serve.
