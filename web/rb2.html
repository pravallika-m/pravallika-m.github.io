Egg fry

  • Time: 20 min
  • serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 3
Salt as required
Turmeric powder pinch
Chilly powder 1 tsp
Coriander powder 1 tbsp

Procedure

Step 1:First boil eggs in water for 10mts
Step 2:Take a pan,add some oil.put boiled eggs in it.
Step 3:Add some salt,turmeric powder,chilly and coriander powders,mix it well.
Step 4:Wait for 5 mts,so that eggs will nicely cook in the mixture.
Step 5:Egg fry is ready to serve.
