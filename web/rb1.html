Tomato Bath

  • Time: 30 min
  • serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
bombay ravva 250 ml
onion 1
tomotoe 2
green Chillies 2
Cumin seeds 1 tsp
chana dal 1 tsp
water 750 ml
Salt as required

PROCEDURE

Step 1: Take a pan.add some oil,add cumin seeds and chana dal.
Step 2: After they splutter add choppped onions,tomotoes and green chillies.
Step 3: After they get mushy,add ravva to it and roast it.
Step 4: In separate pan boil water and then add to this mixture.
Step 5: Mix it well without any lumps.
Step 6: Add salt and curry leaves as required.
Step 7: Tasty tomotoe bath is ready to serve.
