Fish curry

  • Time: 40 min
  • serves 4
Ingredients Quantity
fish pieces 1 bowl
onions 1
tomotoes 2
tamarind paste 1/2 bowl
red chilly powder 2 Tbsp
coriander powder 1 Tbsp
Turmeric powder pinch
menthi powder pinch
garlic 2 cloves
salt as required
Coriander powder 1 tbsp

PROCEDURE

Step 1: Wash the fish pieces and keep it aside.
Step 2: Take a pan,put some oil,add cumin,mustard seeds.
Step 3: Add chilly powder,coriander powder,salt and squeezed tamarind to it.
Step 4: After adding required water let it boil for some time and then add fish pieces to it.
Step 5: Add some menthi powder and garlic to it.boil it for 30 mts.and it is ready to serve.
