Gutti vankaya curry

  • Time: 40 min
  • serves 4
Ingredients Quantity
brinjol 1/2 kg
onions 2
tomotoes 2
ginger garlic paste 4 Tbsp
red chilly powder 2 Tbsp
coriander powder 4 Tbsp
Turmeric powder pinch
menthi powder pinch
mustard seeds 2 tsp
salt as required
curry leaves

PROCEDURE

Step 1: Cut the brinjol like stuffing masala in it.
Step 2: Put the brinjol in water to avoid colr change.
Step 3: Take a pan add some oil,mustard seeds,curry leaves,chopped onions to it.fry until they get mushy.
Step 4: Add ginger garlic paste and fry until raw smell goes.
Step 5: Add choppped tomotoes,salt,turmeric powder,chilly powder and coriander powder.
Step 6: cook it for 10 mts until tomotoes turn mushy.
Step 7: Add the brinjols and cook it for 30 mts so that the mixture coats the brinjol.
Step 8: Add some water to get gravy consistency and cook for 5 more minutes.
Step 9: Gutti vankaya curry is ready to serve.
