Paneer curry

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
paneer 250 ml
onions 2
tomotoes 4
red chilly powder 1 Tbsp
coriander powder 2 Tbsp
turmeric powder pinch
salt as required

Procedure

Step 1: Take a pan.add some oil then add cumin,mustard seeds and let it splutter.
Step 2: Add chopped onions,tomotoes and cook until they get mushy.
Step 3: Add chopped paneer,cook it for 5mts.then add salt,turmeric powder,1 tea spoon red chilly powder.
Step 4: Mix it thoroughly.add some water and cook it for 10 more mts.
Step 5: Paneer curry is ready to serve in rice or rotis.
